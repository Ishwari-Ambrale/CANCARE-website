ABOUT US

Welcome to CANCARE Foundation, an child cancer NGO dedicated to protecting and advocating for the welfare of children suffering from cancer.



Our Vision

Our vision is used to create a future where childhood cancer is no longer threat,where every child's journey
through cancer is met with compassion,hope,and the promise of brighter tommorrow

Our mission

Our mission is to empower young fighters battling cancer by providing them with comprehensive support and resources.
Our organization aims to improve the quality of lifefor these children and their families through medical assistance, emotional support and financial aid

Get Involved

You can get involved by volunteering, donating,and spending time with our loved ones, we can make a difference.
